How does the Gap Analysis work?

Skill Gap Finder uses custom token parsing and regex-based dictionary weights to analyze your resume text against strict requirements for tech roles. It reviews primary skills (core programming/architectures), helper modules (secondary libraries and tools), and soft skill markers to rate profile alignment.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. The Skill Gap Finder runs entirely client-side inside your browser window. Your uploaded PDF files or pasted resume text never traverse network servers. PDF text extraction, keyword parsing, analytics scoring, and charting are computed entirely on your local machine.
